Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Social Help. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and process your personal data when you use our Instagram comment moderation platform.

2. Data We Collect

When you connect your Instagram Professional account using Facebook Login, we collect and store the following information securely in our database:

  • Your Facebook Page ID and Access Token
  • Your linked Instagram Business or Creator Account ID
  • Comments and post data fetched from your Instagram account solely for the purpose of moderation

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is exclusively used to provide our core services: scanning and automatically deleting spam or toxic comments from your Instagram posts based on your configured keywords and toxicity thresholds.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data or your audience's data to any third parties whatsoever, except as required by law.

5. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can disconnect your Instagram account at any time via your dashboard, which immediately removes your Access Tokens from our servers.

Data Deletion Instructions: If you wish to completely delete your account and all associated data, please contact us or delete your account within the app settings. Furthermore, you can revoke our app's access directly from your Facebook settings -> Business Integrations at any time.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@social-help.onrender.com
