Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Social Help ("we", "our", or "us") provides an automated Instagram comment moderation platform that helps creators and businesses manage spam, harmful, or unwanted comments on their Instagram posts.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Social Help application and connect your Instagram Professional account through Facebook Login.

By using Social Help, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Instagram account to Social Help using Facebook Login, we may collect the following information through the official Meta Graph API:

  • Instagram Business or Creator Account ID
  • Facebook Page ID connected to your Instagram account
  • Instagram Post IDs and comment metadata
  • Instagram comments associated with posts that you authorize us to monitor
  • Access tokens required to interact with the Instagram Graph API
  • Basic profile information associated with the connected account

We do not collect passwords or private login credentials for Instagram or Facebook. Authentication occurs securely through Facebook Login.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used strictly to provide the services offered by Social Help, including:

  • Detecting spam or toxic comments on Instagram posts
  • Automatically deleting comments that match configured moderation rules
  • Displaying comment moderation results in your dashboard
  • Allowing users to configure keyword filters and moderation thresholds
  • Maintaining secure communication with the Instagram Graph API

Your information is never used for advertising, marketing profiling, or behavioral tracking.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process user data based on the following lawful grounds:

  • User consent when connecting their Instagram account through Facebook Login
  • Performance of the service requested by the user
  • Compliance with Meta Platform policies and applicable laws

5. Data Storage and Retention

Social Help stores minimal data required for service operation. Access tokens and account identifiers may be stored securely on our servers.

Comment data fetched through the Instagram Graph API may be processed temporarily for moderation purposes and is not permanently stored unless necessary for system logs.

Users may disconnect their Instagram account at any time, which removes stored access tokens and stops all data processing associated with the account.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure server environments
  • Encrypted API communication (HTTPS)
  • Restricted access to stored tokens and identifiers
  • Regular system monitoring

However, no system can be completely secure, and users acknowledge that they use the service at their own discretion.

7. Third-Party Services

Social Help relies on official Meta platforms and APIs including:

  • Facebook Login
  • Instagram Graph API
  • Meta Platform Services

These services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

You can review Meta's privacy policy here: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers or third parties.

Information may only be disclosed if required by law or if necessary to comply with legal obligations.

9. User Rights

Users maintain full control over their data and can:

  • Disconnect their Instagram account at any time
  • Revoke app permissions through Facebook settings
  • Request deletion of stored data

You can remove Social Help access through: Facebook Settings → Business Integrations.

10. Data Deletion

If you would like to request deletion of your account and all associated data, please contact us using the email provided below.

Upon verification, all stored tokens and account identifiers will be removed from our systems.

11. Children's Privacy

Social Help is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect service updates, legal requirements, or platform policy changes.

The updated version will always be available on this page with the revised date.
